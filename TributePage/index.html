Kobe Bryant Tribute

Widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time

1978 - Born in Philadelphia and partly raised in Italy

1996 - NBA drafted and selected by the Charlotte Hornets with the 13th overall pick

1997 - Winner of the Slam Dunk Contest

1997 - Being chosen as an All-Star by his second season

2000 to 2002 - Leading the Lakers together with Saquille O'Neal to three consecutive NBA championships

2005–06 and 2006–07 season - Leading the NBA in scoring

2006 - he scored a career-high 81 points. The second most points scored in a single NBA game, behind Wilt Chamberlain's 100-point game.

2008 and 2012 - He won gold medals in the Olympic Games.

2009 and 2010 - Leading the team to consecutive championships next to his best friend Pau Gasol.

2012–13 season - He suffered a torn achilles tendon at age 34.

2015–16 season - Bryant got retired after the season with 61 points scored in his last game.

2017 - The Lakers retired both his #8 and #24 jerseys, making him the only player in NBA history to have multiple jerseys retired by the same franchise.

2020 - Sadly Bryant died, along with his daughter Gianna and seven others, in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California.

Kobe at a glance

The all-time leading scorer in Lakers history, Bryant was the first guard in NBA history to play 20 seasons. His 18 All-Star designations are the second most all time, and he has the most consecutive appearances as a starter.

Statistics and highlights

  • Points 33,643 (25.0 ppg)
  • Rebounds 7,047 (5.2 rpg)
  • Assists 6,306 (4.7 apg)
